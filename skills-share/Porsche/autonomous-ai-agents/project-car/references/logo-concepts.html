Primary Recommendation

Concept 1: Turbo Neural Core

Dark / Metallic

Core idea: A turbocharger impeller that dissolves into a neural network at the center, representing the fusion of mechanical performance and AI orchestration.

Recommended for app icon, Discord avatar, and marketing. Strong metallic orange/cyan gradient on dark gunmetal background. Extremely scalable.

Best for: Primary branding, dark mode interfaces, apparel.

Minimalist

Concept 2: Mission Circuit

Monochrome with accent

Core idea: A mission-control style circuit board fused with a piston/turbo housing. Clean, technical, and versatile.

Works beautifully in monochrome with a single electric orange accent. Excellent for documentation headers, terminal banners, and light backgrounds.

Best for: Technical docs, GitHub repo, internal tools.

Bold Wordmark

Concept 3: Turbo Springs

Performance Luxury

Core idea: "Turbo Springs" literalized as a coiled spring/turbo element fused with a clean wordmark. Evokes both mechanical performance and the new Discord category name.

Luxury performance feel (think Porsche + SpaceX). Strong presence at large sizes.

Best for: Merch, large signage, hero banners.
